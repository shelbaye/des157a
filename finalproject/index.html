Crossy Bird

Level 1

Navigate your way through the icy waters by jumping on ice bergs until you reach the shore!

Level 2

Oh no! Before reaching the shore, the waves on the beach started to get treacherous. Play as Piplup to help him swim to safety in these violent waters!

Level 3

Piplup must navigate the icy mountains and find his trainer where he last saw her! Use Piplup’s attacks to defend against other wild pokemon until he gets reunited with his trainer!
